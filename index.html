Machine Learning in Business | Kaggle Competition : Santander Customer Satisfaction

A recently concluded Kaggle competition had a prize of $60,000 and was about predicting customer satisfaction: Santander Customer Satisfaction From frontline support teams to C-suites, customer satisfaction is a key measure of success.
